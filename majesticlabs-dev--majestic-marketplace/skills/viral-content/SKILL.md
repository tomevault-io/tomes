# Viral Content Blueprint

Build a platform-specific viral content playbook with psychological hooks, posting schedules, and replicable frameworks based on current algorithm behavior. For zero-budget organic reach.

## Conversation Starter

Use `AskUserQuestion` to gather initial context. Begin by asking:

"I'll help you create a viral content playbook that generates massive organic reach.

Please provide:
1. **Business Type**: What industry/niche are you in?
2. **Target Audience**: Who are you trying to reach? (demographics, interests, pain points)
3. **Primary Platforms**: Where does your audience hang out? (TikTok, LinkedIn, X, Instagram, YouTube, etc.)
4. **Content Capacity**: How much content can you realistically create per week?
5. **Brand Voice**: Serious/professional, casual/funny, edgy, educational?

I'll research what's actually working RIGHT NOW and build a custom viral content system."

## Research Methodology

Use WebSearch extensively to find:
- Currently viral content in the specific niche (last 30 days)
- Platform algorithm updates and what they're prioritizing
- Viral content case studies with engagement metrics
- Zero-budget success stories with specific numbers
- Posting time studies for each platform (2024-2025 data)
- Content format performance comparisons

## Research Standards

- **Recency matters**: Focus on what's working NOW, not 2022 tactics
- **Platform-specific**: Each platform has different viral triggers
- **Numbers over opinions**: Cite engagement rates, view counts, growth metrics
- **Replicable strategies**: Focus on patterns, not one-off flukes

### 3. Psychological Hooks

Triggers that make content irresistible to share:

| Hook Type | Why It Works | Example Phrase | Use When |
|-----------|--------------|----------------|----------|
| Curiosity Gap | Brain needs closure | "The reason most X fail is..." | Educational content |
| Social Currency | Makes sharer look smart | "Only 1% of people know..." | Insider knowledge |
| Emotional Trigger | Feelings drive sharing | "This changed everything..." | Transformation stories |
| Controversy | Debate = engagement | "Unpopular opinion: ..." | Opinion content |
| Specificity | Specific = believable | "How I made $47,832 in..." | Results content |
| Pattern Interrupt | Stops the scroll | "STOP doing [common thing]" | Educational content |
| Identity Signal | Reflects who they are | "If you're a [type]..." | Tribe content |
| FOMO | Fear of missing out | "Everyone's talking about..." | Trend content |
| Reaction Story | Viewer pictures the other person's face | "My [person] said [doubt] until I showed them [result]" | Transformation/product content |

**Hook Formulas:**
- `[Number] + [Unexpected adjective] + [Topic] + [Promise]` — "7 Brutal Truths About Starting a Business Nobody Tells You"
- `[Contrarian statement] + [Proof/reason]` — "Cold outreach is dead. Here's what's working instead."
- `[Person] + [conflict/doubt] → showed them [thing] → they changed their mind` — "My landlord said I can't change anything so I showed her what AI thinks it could look like" (consistently clears 100K+ views on TikTok slideshows)

### 4. One-Day Content Creation System

Create a month's worth of potential viral content in one day:

```markdown
## The Content Sprint Process

### Pre-Sprint Prep (Night Before)
- [ ] Review top 20 posts in your niche from last 30 days
- [ ] List 10 questions your audience asks
- [ ] Note 3 controversial takes you genuinely believe
- [ ] Gather 5 personal stories/results to share

### Hour 1-2: Framework Selection
- Pick 10 frameworks from list
- Assign each to a content piece

### Hour 3-4: Hook Writing
- Write 3 hook options for each piece
- Test hooks against scroll-stopping criteria

### Hour 5-6: Content Creation
- Draft all 10 pieces
- Focus on substance over perfection

### Hour 7: Variation Creation
- Create 3 variations of top 3 pieces
- Different hooks, same core message

### Hour 8: Scheduling & Queue
- Schedule using [free tool]
- Set optimal posting times
- Add to calendar for engagement

**Output:** 10 core pieces + 9 variations = 19 content pieces
**With repurposing:** 30+ posts across platforms
```
