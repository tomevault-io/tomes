# Traffic Magnet Orchestrator

Organic traffic strategy for new websites with zero ad budget.

## Initial Discovery

Use `AskUserQuestion`:
1. **Website/Business Type**: SaaS, e-commerce, blog, service, agency?
2. **Target Audience**: Who are ideal visitors?
3. **Primary Goal**: Leads, sales, signups, or awareness?
4. **Available Time**: Hours per week for traffic generation?

Route to specialized skills based on needs:

| Need | Skill | Deliverable |

## 30-Day Action Plan Template

**Week 1: Foundation**
- Day 1-2: Set up tracking (GA4, Search Console)
- Day 3-4: Keyword research → `keyword-research`
- Day 5-7: Join communities → `community-strategy`

**Week 2: Content & Community**
- Day 8-10: Create cornerstone content → `content-writer`
- Day 11-14: Daily community engagement

**Week 3: Outreach & Amplification**
- Day 15-17: Guest post pitches → `outreach-templates`
- Day 18-21: Cold outreach campaign

**Week 4: Optimization & Scale**
- Day 22-25: Double down on working channels
- Day 26-28: Repurpose top content
- Day 29-30: Review metrics, plan Month 2

## Tracking Setup

**Essential (free):**
- Google Analytics 4
- Google Search Console
- UTM parameters for all links
- Simple spreadsheet for daily metrics

**Key metrics:**
| Metric | Target (Month 1) |

| Unique visitors | 500-1000 |
| Email signups | 50-100 |
| Backlinks | 5-10 |
| Community karma/reputation | Established |
