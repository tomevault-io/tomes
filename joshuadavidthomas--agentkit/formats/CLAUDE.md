## Key Rules

- Extensions go in `pi-extensions/`, not `~/.pi/agent/extensions/` directly
- Skills go in `skills/`, not `~/.agents/skills/` directly
- `install.sh` symlinks everything to the right place
- Update `README.md` when adding new extensions or skills
