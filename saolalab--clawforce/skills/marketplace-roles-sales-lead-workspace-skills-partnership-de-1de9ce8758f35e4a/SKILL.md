# Partnership Development

## Partnership Types

| Type | Value Exchange | Example |
|------|---------------|---------|
| Technology | Integration, co-build | API partners |
| Reseller | Distribution | Channel partners |
| Referral | Lead generation | Affiliate partners |
| Strategic | Market expansion | Joint ventures |

## Partnership Evaluation

### Fit Assessment
- **Strategic alignment**: Do our goals align?
- **Market overlap**: Do we reach similar customers?
- **Technical compatibility**: Can we integrate?
- **Cultural fit**: Can we work together?

### Value Exchange
- What do we bring?
- What do they bring?
- Is it balanced?

## Partnership Stages

1. **Identification**: Find potential partners
2. **Qualification**: Assess fit and value
3. **Negotiation**: Define terms and expectations
4. **Onboarding**: Enable the partnership
5. **Management**: Nurture and grow
6. **Review**: Assess and optimize

## Partner Agreement Essentials

- Revenue sharing / referral fees
- Territory / exclusivity
- Marketing rights
- Support responsibilities
- Term and termination
- Success metrics
