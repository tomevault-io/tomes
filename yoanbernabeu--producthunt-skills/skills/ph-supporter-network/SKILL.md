# Product Hunt Supporter Network

This skill helps you build, organize, and activate a network of supporters who will genuinely engage with your Product Hunt launch.

## When to Use This Skill

- Building your pre-launch supporter list
- Categorizing supporters by relationship strength
- Creating outreach templates for different groups
- Coordinating launch day support
- Maximizing authentic engagement

## Why Supporter Networks Matter

### The Reality
- Top launches have 50-200+ active supporters
- Algorithm weighs account quality heavily
- Genuine engagement > raw numbers
- Comments matter as much as upvotes

### What Makes Good Support
- ✓ Actual Product Hunt users (not new accounts)
- ✓ Thoughtful comments (not just "Great!")
- ✓ Authentic interest in your product
- ✓ Staggered engagement (not all at once)

## Building Your Supporter List

### Categories of Supporters

#### Tier 1: Inner Circle (Target: 20-50)
**Who:**
- Co-founders, team members
- Close friends and family (who use PH)
- Early beta users
- Advisors and investors

**Characteristics:**
- Will definitely support
- High engagement quality
- Personal relationship

---

#### Tier 2: Warm Network (Target: 50-100)
**Who:**
- Professional connections
- Industry peers
- Past colleagues
- Active community members

**Characteristics:**
- Likely to support if asked
- Understand your product
- Have PH accounts

---

#### Tier 3: Extended Network (Target: 100-300)
**Who:**
- Newsletter subscribers
- Social media followers
- Past customers
- One-time interactions

**Characteristics:**
- May support if convenient
- Less personal connection
- Varying PH activity levels

---

#### Tier 4: Reciprocal (Target: 20-50)
**Who:**
- Other makers you've supported
- Launch swap partners
- Community reciprocity

**Characteristics:**
- Mutual benefit relationship
- Understand launch dynamics
- Active PH users

## Supporter Qualification

### Ideal Supporter Profile
- ✓ Has Product Hunt account
- ✓ Account is 30+ days old
- ✓ Has some activity history
- ✓ Genuinely interested in your product category
- ✓ Can write thoughtful comment

### Red Flags (Don't Pursue)
- ❌ No PH account and unwilling to create
- ❌ Would create account just for your launch
- ❌ No genuine interest in your product
- ❌ Only offering transactional exchange
- ❌ History of spam behavior

## Pre-Launch Relationship Building

### Timeline

**8-12 Weeks Before:**
- Start mapping your network
- Identify potential supporters
- Begin meaningful interactions

**4-6 Weeks Before:**
- Reach out to key supporters
- Share early access/previews
- Get feedback and buy-in

**2-3 Weeks Before:**
- Confirm supporter availability
- Share launch details
- Provide any needed resources

**1 Week Before:**
- Final confirmation
- Send calendar invites
- Share exact timing

**1 Day Before:**
- Last reminder
- Share comment ideas
- Express gratitude

## Launch Day Coordination

### Supporter Briefing Document

Prepare a document to share with supporters:

```
📋 [PRODUCT] LAUNCH DAY GUIDE

LAUNCH DATE: [Date]
LAUNCH TIME: 12:01 AM PST

LINK: [Will be shared at launch]

HOW TO SUPPORT:

1️⃣ VISIT the Product Hunt page (link above)

2️⃣ UPVOTE if you genuinely think it's useful

3️⃣ COMMENT with your honest thoughts:
   - What you like about it
   - Your use case
   - A question for us
   - Constructive feedback

💡 COMMENT IDEAS:
- "[Feature] is exactly what I needed for [use case]"
- "Love how this solves [problem]. Have you considered [idea]?"
- "Been using the beta and [personal experience]"
- [Any specific prompts relevant to your product]

⚠️ IMPORTANT:
- Don't just write "Great product!" (low quality)
- Be genuine — fake enthusiasm is obvious
- Don't all comment at the same time
- Reply to other comments if you have thoughts

🕐 TIMING GUIDE:
If you're in:
- US West Coast: Check at midnight
- US East Coast: Morning is fine
- Europe: Afternoon works
- Asia/Pacific: Evening is great

THANK YOU! 🙏

Even just taking a look means the world to me.
```

### Staggered Activation

Don't have everyone engage at once:

| Wave | Time (PST) | Who | Action |
|------|------------|-----|--------|
| 1 | 12:01 AM | Inner Circle A | Upvote + Comment |
| 2 | 2:00 AM | Inner Circle B | Upvote + Comment |
| 3 | 5:00 AM | Warm Network (EU) | Upvote + Comment |
| 4 | 8:00 AM | Warm Network (US East) | Upvote + Comment |
| 5 | 11:00 AM | Extended Network | Upvote |
| 6 | 2:00 PM | Reciprocal | Upvote + Comment |
| 7 | 6:00 PM | Reminder to non-engagers | Upvote |
