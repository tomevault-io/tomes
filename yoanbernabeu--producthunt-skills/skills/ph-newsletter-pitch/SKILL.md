# Product Hunt Newsletter Pitch

This skill helps you get featured in Product Hunt's newsletters, extending your launch visibility beyond launch day.

## When to Use This Skill

- Before launch: Planning for newsletter inclusion
- During launch: Pitching to editorial
- After launch: Follow-up pitches
- Anytime: Understanding newsletter strategy

## Product Hunt Newsletters

### Newsletter Types

**The Leaderboard (Daily)**
- Sent Monday-Friday
- Top 10 launches of the day
- Quick snapshot format
- Highest reach

**The Roundup (Sunday)**
- Week's top products
- Community discussions
- Long-form content
- Curated selection

**The Frontier (Tuesday)**
- AI-focused insights
- Top AI launches
- Founder features
- Specialized audience

**Developer Tools Edition**
- Developer-focused products
- Technical audience
- Specific criteria

### Newsletter Reach

- Sent to millions of subscribers
- High open rates (tech audience)
- Drives significant traffic
- Extends launch visibility

## Getting Featured

### Automatic Inclusion

**Daily Newsletter (The Leaderboard):**
- Top 10 products automatically included
- Based on ranking at end of day
- No pitch needed

### Editorial Selection

**Weekly & Specialty Newsletters:**
- Editorially curated
- Based on interest/uniqueness
- Can be pitched
- Quality over ranking

## What Editors Look For

### Key Criteria (from PH Editorial)

**1. Uniqueness**
- Doesn't need to be revolutionary
- Must do something different
- Not "another [category] tool"
- Clear differentiation

**2. Visual Quality**
- Strong gallery images
- Clear screenshots
- Compelling thumbnail
- Professional presentation

**3. Clear Value Proposition**
- Easy to understand
- Obvious benefit
- Specific use case
- Not vague/generic

**4. Interesting Story**
- Why it was built
- Who it's for
- What problem it solves
- Maker passion

### Red Flags (Won't Get Featured)

- Generic products
- Poor visual quality
- Unclear purpose
- Low-quality listing
- Obvious cash grabs
- Clones without innovation

## Crafting Your Pitch

### When to Pitch

**Proactive Pitch:**
- Day of launch (morning)
- Before newsletter deadline (7 AM PT)

**Reactive Pitch:**
- After launch, for future editions
- For specialty newsletters

### Pitch Email Template

```
To: editorial@producthunt.co
Subject: [Product Name] — [Unique angle] | Live on PH today

Hi PH Editorial Team,

[Product Name] just went live on Product Hunt:
[Link]

WHAT IT IS:
[One sentence description]

WHAT MAKES IT UNIQUE:
[Key differentiator — what's new/different]

WHO IT'S FOR:
[Specific target audience]

WHY NOW:
[Timeliness or relevance]

GALLERY HIGHLIGHTS:
• Image 1: [What it shows]
• Video: [Brief description]

Happy to provide any additional info!

Best,
[Your Name]
[Your Title]
[Product URL]
```

### Pitch Best Practices

**Do:**
- Keep it brief (skimmable)
- Lead with uniqueness
- Include direct PH link
- Highlight visual assets
- Be professional

**Don't:**
- Write a novel
- Beg for inclusion
- Exaggerate claims
- Send multiple follow-ups
- Be pushy

## Optimizing for Newsletter

### Before Launch

**Gallery Preparation:**
- First image must be compelling
- Clear product shots
- Readable at small sizes
- Video if possible

**Listing Quality:**
- Strong tagline
- Clear description
- Complete profile
- No typos

### Day of Launch

**Timing:**
- Launch at 12:01 AM PT
- Newsletter compiles ~7 AM PT
- Early morning performance matters

**Engagement:**
- Active comments
- Quality discussions
- Maker presence
